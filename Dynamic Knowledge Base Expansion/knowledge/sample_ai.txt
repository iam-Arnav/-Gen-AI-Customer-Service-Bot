Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence by machines.

Machine Learning is a subset of AI that allows computers to learn from data.

Deep Learning is a subset of Machine Learning that uses neural networks.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) enables computers to understand and generate human language.

Computer Vision allows machines to interpret images and videos.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combines a language model with a vector database to answer questions from external documents.

FAISS is a vector similarity search library used to store and retrieve embeddings efficiently.

Sentence Transformers convert text into numerical embeddings for semantic search.

Gemini is Google's family of large language models used for text generation and question answering.
